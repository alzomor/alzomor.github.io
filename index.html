How SmartPark Works

A complete parking management system for users and operators

For Users

1

Park Your Vehicle

Park your vehicle in a designated parking area.

2

Select Vehicle

Choose from your registered license plates or add a new vehicle to the system.

3

Set Duration

Select how long you plan to park and see the estimated cost before confirming.

4

Pay for Parking

Complete payment using your preferred method and receive a digital confirmation.

5

Get Reminders

Receive notifications before your parking session expires with options to extend if needed.

For Parking Operators

1

Scan License Plates

Operators use the companion app to scan vehicle license plates in parking areas.

2

Verify Payment Status

The system automatically checks if the vehicle owner has paid for parking.

3

Issue Penalties

If payment is not found, the system generates a penalty notice for the vehicle owner.

4

Send Notifications

Vehicle owners receive immediate notification of penalties through the app.
